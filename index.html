Symposium Patavinum

A student-organized seminar series where we explore mathematics, physics, and original research ideas together. Every other month, one of us presents a topic of interest followed by open discussion.

About Us

Symposium Patavinum is a small community of students passionate about deep ideas in mathematics and theoretical physics. Our goal is to create a relaxed environment where we can present topics we care about and share original research ideas. The group was founded in October 2024.
